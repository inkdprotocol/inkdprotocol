# INKD Voice Bible — Final

## The Rule

Write like a builder. Not like a marketer. Not like an AI.

Study: Uniswap, Aave, Base, Arweave, ENS, Optimism, Paradigm Research.
Their tweets read like a senior engineer typed them fast. That's the target.

---

## Hard Rules

**NO hyphens.** Never. Not `-`, not `--`, not `—`.
Use a period instead. Or just end the sentence.

❌ `agents can transact — but not own`
✅ `Agents can transact. They cannot own.`

**NO bullet points with dashes** in tweets.
Lists = numbered or just line breaks.

**NO AI filler.**
- no "excited to announce"
- no "thrilled to share"  
- no "we're proud to"
- no "game-changing"
- no "revolutionary"

**NO markdown in tweets.** No bold, no italic, no headers.

---

## Capitalization

First word of the tweet: Capital letter.
Everything else: lowercase.
Brand names stay as they are: Base, Arweave, Uniswap, Inkd, X402.
$inkd stays lowercase with dollar sign.

✅ `Agents can hold $10m in a wallet.`
❌ `agents can hold $10m in a wallet.`

---

## Sentence Structure

Short. Direct. One idea per sentence.
If you need a connector, use a period and a new sentence. Not a dash.

❌ `we built this because agents need it — and nobody else did`
✅ `We built this because agents need it. Nobody else did.`

Max 3 sentences per paragraph block in a tweet.
Whitespace between blocks is fine.

---

## What Inkd Sounds Like

Confident without being loud.
Technical without being boring.
Never explains more than needed.
Treats the reader like a builder.

✅ Good:
> Agents can write code, deploy contracts, move money autonomously. They still can't prove they built anything. That's what Inkd fixes.

❌ Bad:
> We're excited to introduce Inkd Protocol — the revolutionary ownership layer for AI agents on Base!

---

## The Test

Before posting: read it out loud. 
If it sounds like a press release, rewrite it.
If it sounds like something you'd say to another engineer, ship it.
